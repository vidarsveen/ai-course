The Hierarchy of AI

Visualizing the Relationship Between AI, ML, and Generative AI

Visualizing the Relationship

This diagram shows the hierarchy. AI is the broadest field. Machine Learning is a *type* of AI. Generative AI is a specific *type* of Machine Learning.

Artificial Intelligence (AI)

The broad field of simulating human intelligence.

Machine Learning (ML)

A subfield of AI that learns from data.

Generative AI

A subfield of ML that creates new content.
